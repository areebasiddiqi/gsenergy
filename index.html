04 — How we work

From signal
to savings.

Every engagement runs on the same disciplined cadence — diagnose first, design once, verify always. Below is what a typical retrofit looks like in motion.

STEP 01

Discover

Site walks, utility data review, and stakeholder interviews to map the actual operation — not the as-designed one.

STEP 02

Diagnose

ASHRAE-aligned analysis identifies the specific equipment, sequences, and code gaps driving cost and risk.

STEP 03

Design & Implement

We specify retrofits, oversee execution, and write functional tests that hold contractors to a measurable standard.

STEP 04

Verify

M&V across a full operating year, plus LL97 reporting support — so the savings on paper match the meter.
